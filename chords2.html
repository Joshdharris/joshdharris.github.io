Minor Chords

Check it. These are minor chords.

Theyre like the major chords but the middle note has been taken down by a half-step (e.g: the C chord (C E G) becomes Cm (C D♭ G))

The Am Chord

This the A minor chord. We write it as Am. You can hover over it to see how it compares to a regular A chord.
